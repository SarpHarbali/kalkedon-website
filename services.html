Service 01

Bulk Logistics

ISO tank containers are designed for intermodal transportation of liquid and gas chemicals with the highest safety by sea, rail and road. Kalkedon has operated as a tank container fleet operator since 1998, providing multimodal transport to the chemical industry.

As an ISO Tank Operator, Kalkedon applies its own standards to be able to offer quality beyond food grade, ensuring every shipment meets or exceeds the strictest international safety requirements.

Service 02

Freight Forwarding

With a global network and strong partnerships with major air, ocean and road carriers, international freight forwarding is one of our core services.

Sea Freight Services

We have many sea freight specialists throughout our worldwide network, ready to help you find the right solution, customized to meet your unique requirements. Regardless of the size of your business or your location, we will be able to meet your requirements along with tailor-made rates to ensure value.

  • Full Container Load (FCL) and Less Container Load (LCL)
  • Worldwide coverage with reliable shipping lines
  • Slot contracts ensuring competitive rates
  • Regular inbound and outbound sailings
  • Door-to-door service
  • Multi-modal options
  • Hazardous Goods Transportation (IMCO)
  • Customs clearance and warehousing

Road Freight Services

With well-established routes throughout the Caspian Region & North Africa and a comprehensive strategic partner network, Kalkedon Logistics offers daily services to all corners of the Caspian Region & North Africa and beyond. As an R2 License holder, our road freight operations meet the highest regulatory standards.

  • R2 License holder
  • Daily FTL and LTL departures from Europe to Turkey and beyond
  • Specialized routing to Middle East and Caucasian countries
  • Hazardous Goods Transportation (LTL/FTL)
  • Temperature-controlled service (FTL)
  • Hanging garments service (FTL)

Air Freight Services

We have a dedicated team of professionals who have delivered tremendous growth in the international air freight sector across the globe. Our global network of international freight forwarders fulfills your air freight requirements effectively. You are our top priority — we do our best to build and maintain trust.

  • Worldwide coverage with reliable airlines
  • Just-in-time delivery
  • Door-to-door service
  • Multi-modal capabilities
  • Full customs clearance
Service 03

Caspian Freight Bridge

Istanbul is the natural logistic bridge connecting the world to Central Asia, the Balkans and the Middle East. This geographic advantage inspired Kalkedon to specialize in transit services and develop this signature product: the Caspian Freight Bridge.

Service Advantages
  • Multimodal combinations: sea + rail, road + rail, sea + road
  • Cross-docking between containers and railcars
  • Direct railway contracts
  • Daily wagon and container tracking
  • Warehouse services in Azerbaijan, Kazakhstan, and Turkmenistan
