Transport Modes

Freight Forwarding in Detail

With a global network and strong partnerships with major air, ocean and road carriers, international freight forwarding is one of our core services.

Sea Freight

FCL/LCL, door-to-door, multi-modal, and Hazardous Goods (IMCO). Worldwide coverage with tailor-made rates. Reliable shipping lines with slot contracts and regular inbound/outbound sailings.

Road Freight

R2 License holder. Daily FTL and LTL departures from Europe to Turkey and beyond, with specialized routing to Middle East and Caucasian countries. Temperature-controlled and Hazardous Goods available.

Air Freight

Worldwide coverage with reliable airlines, just-in-time delivery, door-to-door service and full customs clearance. A dedicated team delivering your air freight requirements effectively.
