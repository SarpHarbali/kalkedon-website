Our Story

A Specialist Built for Chemical Logistics

Established in 1998 as a tank container fleet operator, Kalkedon was built from the ground up to serve one of the most demanding sectors in global trade: the chemical and oil industries. From day one, the focus was on multimodal transport — combining sea, rail, and road to move liquid and gas chemicals safely across international borders.

Over the years, Kalkedon expanded its capabilities to become a full-service international logistics specialist, while never losing its core expertise in bulk chemicals. Today, the company operates its own fleet of ISO tank containers meeting the highest international safety standards.
